# IPL Sponsorship ROI — A Data-Driven Investment Framework

## Business Problem
IPL sponsorship is a ₹4,000+ crore industry, but most brand managers 
choose teams based on popularity rather than performance data. This 
project builds a Sponsor Value Score for every IPL team — combining 
win rate, match exposure, and player brand equity — to answer:

> "Which team, venue and timing strategy delivers the highest ROI 
> for a brand's IPL sponsorship investment?"

## Key Recommendations
1. Chennai Super Kings leads the Sponsor Value Score at 99.8 points — 
   2.1% ahead of second-ranked Royal Challengers Bangalore. Recommended as primary 
   partner for any brand prioritising performance equity.

2. Eden Gardens, Wankhede Stadium and M Chinnaswamy Stadium collectively host 29.22% of all IPL matches 
   and span India's top metro markets — optimal for on-ground 
   activation budgets.

3. The opening 2 weeks of the IPL season show peak fan 
   engagement — the most cost-efficient window for campaign launches.

4. AB de Villiers leads all players in Man of the Match 
   awards (24 across 11 seasons) — a natural co-branding 
   opportunity for brands wanting earned media beyond paid exposure.

## Methodology
Built a composite Sponsor Value Score weighted as:
- 40% win rate — performance consistency and brand equity transfer
- 35% total matches played — raw broadcast exposure volume  
- 25% Man of the Match frequency — star power and premium airtime

This formula reflects priorities of an FMCG or consumer brand 
seeking broad reach and consistent association over prestige alone.
